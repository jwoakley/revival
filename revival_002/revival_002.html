Many years ago a crude substance employed in the manufacture of a certain product became quite scarce and its price excessive. So a junior engaged in the laboratory suggested an ingredient costing less and easier to get. This young man argued that by thus altering the formula a saving would be effected and nobody would know about it. The manufacturer explained that he would not hesitate to change the formula if an improvement could be effected, but the master formula used in every worthy business was personal honor and absolute reliability plus business integrity and that was one formula which would not be changed.
